# Third Interlude

## Synopsis

Curisoty has found something from the records he wants the team's attention on. Once the team shows up, he tells it will require some more digging to verify, and he doesn't want to say more in case he's wrong.

What they discover is a factory for Androids that look like the less advanced opponents they've fought. The team learns these "lesser" Androids were mass-produced in the image of the Androids of old!

But that's not what Curiosity worried about. Rather, they find a secretive inner chamber further in labeled "Project Lucifer". The chamber is small, but lined up with pods much like the ones they woke up in. It also houses a smaller data center, much like those at the university, with version control manager and state logs. Going by these records, the Custodians managed to fuse units capable of piloting Light Knight with the characters of the ancient Android, which has been going on for thousands of iterations.

And they are the end results of this project! Or, at least, the current iteration. Were these Custodians to access these systems, they could change their very minds at will.

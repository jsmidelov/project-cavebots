# Fixed beats during Chapter 2

These come in sequential order between the anthology stories to bring the story back on track and remind the player of the story's context, and raise the stakes.

## After first branch

The team discovers the fortress holds a garage with vehicles and cycles. They don't have nearly enough power to start more than the road-laying robot and a vehicle, but even that brings all towns they've reached so much closer.

Still, there are way too many vehicles here for their needs, and not all of them seems made to stay on solid ground. Why is such a depot within the mountain?

## After second branch

The team digs deeper into the fortress, and discovers a university with a library.

They discover a data center mostly on stand-by, but with Sophia's computer possession skills they figure out the few running processes are running the software of the still active androids in the field. As it happens, they also store their locations, so they can be tracked down.

Going by the books on history and politics, the extraterrestial colonies not only survived the Man-Machine War, but are still around as the Stellar Federation. By the most recent (yet very out_date_ed) newspapers, they still orbit the world in a menacing way. The mysterious Light Knights are their machines, sent planetside from orbit as a show of force. If whatever they want to prevent from happening does happen, they were clearly signaling they could do a lot of damage.

Which means the same Light Knights are still around and, to get their desired peace, they must somehow be driven off. Further, the Custodians (which are the Golems during the Arcane Age, but I'm not sure if the Androids know that yet) allude to a counter they ominously call "Project Lucifer". Curisoty decides to stay in the library and put together whatever he can from what's available.

## After third branch

Curisoty has found something from the records he wants the team's attention on. Once the team shows up, he tells it will require some more digging to verify, and he doesn't want to say more in case he's wrong.

What they discover is a factory for Androids that look like the less advanced opponents they've fought. The team learns these "lesser" Androids were mass-produced in the image of the Androids of old!

But that's not what Curiosity worried about. Rather, they find a secretive inner chamber further in labeled "Project Lucifer". The chamber is small, but lined up with pods much like the ones they woke up in. It also houses a smaller data center, much like those at the university, with version control manager and state logs. Going by these records, the Custodians managed to fuse units capable of piloting Light Knight with the characters of the ancient Android, which has been going on for thousands of iterations.

And they are the end results of this project! Or, at least, the current iteration. Were these Custodians to access these systems, they could change their very minds at will.

## After all four (?) branches

Begin the finale!

The team is assembled, the fort is explored, the truth is out. Time to face the golems.

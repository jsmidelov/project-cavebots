# Second Interlude

## Synopsis

The team digs deeper into the fortress, and discovers a university with a library.

They discover a data center mostly on stand-by, but with Sophia's computer possession skills they figure out the few running processes are running the software of the still active androids in the field. As it happens, they also store their locations, so they can be tracked down.

Going by the books on history and politics, the extraterrestial colonies not only survived the Man-Machine War, but are still around as the Stellar Federation. By the most recent (yet very out_date_ed) newspapers, they still orbit the world in a menacing way. The mysterious Light Knights are their machines, sent planetside from orbit as a show of force. If whatever they want to prevent from happening does happen, they were clearly signaling they could do a lot of damage.

Which means the same Light Knights are still around and, to get their desired peace, they must somehow be driven off. Further, the Custodians (which are the Golems during the Arcane Age, but I'm not sure if the Androids know that yet) allude to a counter they ominously call "Project Lucifer". Curisoty decides to stay in the library and put together whatever he can from what's available.
